Who is this for?

Target audience

The target audience is anyone who wants to know the current or future weather. The website will help anyone who wants to get ahead of the climate.

Persona #1

Rex

Like any typical clone, Rex has a high degree of web literacy and often goes online. He is planning to go patroling with his troop on Kamino today.

He is anxious about the weather and checks every website enthusiastically. He wants a website where it shows more than a current temperature. He wants a site that is user-friendly so he can easily check the weather at any time.

Persona #2

Kaminoans

Kaminoans are the native race of Kamnio. A typical Kaminoan spends their day working on cloning, clone training, and maintaining a good work-life balance.

An average Kaminoan knows that Kamino's weather is always changing. So, before heaing to work, they check KaminoWeather.com.

Any scenarios?

Scenarios

  1. It's 9 a.m, and the average Kamino citizen needs to get to their office. However, it's gloomy outside. One check of the weather website makes sure they know if it's going to rain or not.
  2. Rex has a speeder race with Ralph tonight. It's been raining non-stop since yesterday, so he frequently checks the weather website.
  3. Clone 119 has planned a long sea training trip with this troop. They want to make sure the weather is safe all week before going.
  4. Clone 2319 and his six troopers are going to be flight traning this week. Clone 2319 checks the weather to make sure his troop isn't in danger.
  5. Kamilia and her husband Kam are going to Alderaan for their honeymoon. Kam wants to see how the weather will affect their flight, so he checks KaminoWeather.com.
